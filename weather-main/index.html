Weather Q&A

Why does humidity affect how hot it feels?

High humidity slows sweat evaporation, reducing the body’s cooling and making temperatures feel hotter.

What causes sudden afternoon thunderstorms?

Surface heating creates rising moist air. As it cools aloft, clouds build rapidly; instability and moisture trigger storms.

Is a lower pressure always a sign of rain?

Falling pressure often precedes unsettled weather, but local factors and fronts determine actual rainfall.
